Commands to perform Operations

1.Check Weather by City Name:

[python weather_app.py CITY_NAME]
Replace CITY_NAME with the name of the city for which you want to check the weather.

2.Add City to Favorites:

[python weather_app.py -a CITY_NAME]
Replace CITY_NAME with the name of the city you want to add to favorites.

3.Remove City from Favorites:

[python weather_app.py -r CITY_NAME]
Replace CITY_NAME with the name of the city you want to remove from favorites.

4.List Favorite Cities:

[python weather_app.py -c]
This command will list all the favorite cities that you have added.
